Fairfield Inn & Suites by Marriott Vernon

5300 Anderson Way, Vernon, BC CA
Return to Search ResultsNew Search

Hotel Description

Property Location
Located in Vernon, Fairfield Inn & Suites by Marriott Vernon is in the business district and minutes from Village Green Mall and Village Green Centre. This hotel is within close proximity of Kalamalka Lake Provincial Park and Polson Park.

Rooms
Make yourself at home in one of the 98 air-conditioned rooms featuring refrigerators and LED televisions. Complimentary wired and wireless Internet access keeps you connected, and digital programming provides entertainment. Private bathrooms with shower/tub combinations feature designer toiletries and hair dryers. Conveniences include desks and coffee/tea makers, as well as phones with free local calls.

Amenities
Enjoy a range of recreational amenities, including an indoor pool, a spa tub, and a 24-hour fitness center. Additional amenities at this hotel include complimentary wireless Internet access, a fireplace in the lobby, and tour/ticket assistance.

Dining
Grab a bite from a grocery/convenience store serving guests of Fairfield Inn & Suites by Marriott Vernon.

Business, Other Amenities
Featured amenities include complimentary high-speed (wired) Internet access, a 24-hour business center, and express check-out. Planning an event in Vernon? This hotel has facilities measuring 1400 square feet (126 square meters), including a meeting room. Guests may use a roundtrip airport shuttle for a surcharge, and free self parking is available onsite.
