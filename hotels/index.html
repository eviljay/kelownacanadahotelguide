Delta Grand Okanagan Resort and Conference Center

Delta Grand Okanagan Resort and Conference Center

Property Location
With a stay at Delta Grand Okanagan Resort and Conference Center, you'll be centrally located in Kelowna, steps from Kelowna Art Gallery and Prospera Place. This casino resort is within close proximity of Waterfront Park and Okanagan Military Museum.

Rooms
Make yourself at home in one of the 320 air-conditioned guestrooms. Complimentary wired and wireless Internet access keeps you connected, and pay movies provides entertainment. Private bathrooms have complimentary toiletries and hair dryers. Conveniences include desks and coffee/tea makers, as well as cordless phones with voice mail.

Rec, Spa, Premium Amenities

Additional amenities include complimentary wireless Internet access, gift shops/newsstands, and a hair salon.

Dining
Enjoy a meal at a restaurant, or stay in and take advantage of the resort's 24-hour room service. Quench your thirst with your favorite drink at a bar/lounge. Breakfast is available for a fee.

Business, Other Amenities

Coast Capri Hotel

Coast Capri Hotel

1171 Harvey Ave -- Kelowna, BC

Property Location
Coast Capri Hotel is centrally located in Kelowna, walking distance from Capri Mall and close to Okanagan Military Museum. This family-friendly hotel is within close proximity of Guisachan Heritage Park and Kasagui Gardens.

Rooms
Make yourself at home in one of the 185 air-conditioned rooms featuring refrigerators and iPod docking stations. Your pillowtop bed comes with Egyptian cotton sheets. Relax and take in city and pool views from the privacy of your room. Complimentary wireless Internet access keeps you connected, and cable programming is available for your entertainment. Private bathrooms with shower/tub combinations feature makeup/shaving mirrors and complimentary toiletries.

Rec, Spa, Premium Amenities
Pamper yourself with a visit to the spa, which offers massages, body treatments, and facials. You can take advantage of recreational amenities such as an outdoor pool, a spa tub, and a 24-hour fitness facility. Additional features include complimentary wireless Internet access, concierge services, and gift shops/newsstands. Hop aboard the complimentary shuttle and get to the casino or shopping center in no time.

Dining
Grab a bite to eat at the hotel's restaurant, which features a bar, or stay in and take advantage of 24-hour room service. Relax with your favorite drink at a bar/lounge or a poolside bar.

Business, Other Amenities
Featured amenities include a 24-hour business center, limo/town car service, and audiovisual equipment. Planning an event in Kelowna? This hotel has 15000 square feet (1394 square meters) of space consisting of a conference center, conference/meeting rooms, and small meeting rooms. A roundtrip airport shuttle is complimentary at scheduled times.

Playa Del Sol Resort

654 Cook Rd -- Kelowna, BC

Property Location
With a stay at Playa Del Sol Resort in Kelowna (Kelowna - West Kelowna), you'll be minutes from Rotary Beach Park and close to H2O Adventure and Fitness Centre. This 4-star condominium resort is within close proximity of Tantalus Vineyards and Guisachan Heritage Park.

Rooms
Make yourself at home in one of the 280 individually decorated guestrooms, featuring kitchens with full-sized refrigerators/freezers and stovetops. Your bed comes with cotton sheets, and all rooms are furnished with sofa beds. Rooms have private balconies where you can take in city and lake views. wired and wireless Internet access is complimentary, while DVD players and cable programming provide entertainment.

Rec, Spa, Premium Amenities
Pamper yourself with a visit to the spa, which offers body treatments and facials. You're sure to appreciate the recreational amenities, including a health club, an outdoor pool, and a spa tub. This condominium resort also features complimentary wireless Internet access, a hair salon, and a pool table.

Dining
Grab a bite to eat at one of the condominium resort's dining establishments, which include 2 restaurants and a coffee shop/café. Quench your thirst with your favorite drink at a bar/lounge.

Business, Other Amenities
Featured amenities include complimentary high-speed (wired) Internet access, express check-in, and express check-out. Free parking is available onsite.
