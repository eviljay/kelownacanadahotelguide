Myra Canyon Trestle Trail Information and Hotels

Myra Canyon Trestle Trail
Central Okanagan, BC CA
Myra Canyon Trestle Trail

Located in Myra-Bellevue Provincial Park, southeast of Kelowna, this trestle trail is quite popular with visitors! Wildfires in 2003 produced hazards in the area, but it has recovered nicely. Hike or bike the trail to see the canyon, Bellevue Creek, Angel springs and the Kettle Valley Railway, some of which is part of the Trans Canada Trail. Horseback riding is permitted and is a wonderful way to experience the beautiful scenery. Look for the 14 viewpoint benches along the trail, each of which offers a unique and fabulous vista. Wildlife sightings include grizzy bear, elk, deer, moose, cougar, mountain goats and a great variety of bird species.
