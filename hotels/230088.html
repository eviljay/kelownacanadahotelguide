Inn at Big White

5375 Big White Road, Kelowna, BC CA
Return to Search ResultsNew Search

Hotel Description

Property Location
Located in Kelowna (Big White), Inn at Big White is minutes from Big White Ski Resort and Bullet Express. This ski resort is within close proximity of Ridge Rocket Express.

Rooms
Treat yourself to a stay in one of the 100 guestrooms, featuring fireplaces. Complimentary wireless Internet access is available to keep you connected. Conveniences include phones, as well as complimentary newspapers and refrigerators.

Amenities
Take time to pamper yourself with a visit to the full-service spa. You're sure to appreciate the convenience of ski-in/ski-out access at this resort, which also features an outdoor pool and a spa tub. Additional amenities include complimentary wireless Internet access, concierge services, and babysitting/childcare (surcharge). Guests can catch a ride to nearby destinations on the complimentary area shuttle.

Dining
Enjoy a meal at a restaurant, or stay in and take advantage of the resort's room service (during limited hours). Quench your thirst with your favorite drink at a bar/lounge.

Business, Other Amenities
Featured amenities include complimentary newspapers in the lobby, a 24-hour front desk, and luggage storage. A roundtrip airport shuttle is provided for a surcharge (available 24 hours), and free self parking is available onsite.
