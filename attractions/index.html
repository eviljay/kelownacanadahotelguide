Kelowna British Columbia Attractions

Ballet Kelowna

There's nothing more beautiful than a ballet performance, and you are in luck! Ballet Kelowna productions are offered at the Kelowna Community Theatre, so plan to attend while you are visiting.

Ballet Kelowna

Get an education about the wine industry in the Okanagan Valley at the BC Wine Museum - and have a taste of the grape as well!

Big White Ski Resort

Big White Ski Resort offers much more than just skiing - try dog sledding, snowmobility and tubing, too! The resort boasts an outstanding terrain park as well as the Village Centre Mall and a plethora of dining and pub options.

Big White Ski Resort

Fintry Provincial Park is worth the drive! It offers visitors the opportunity to explore a national heritage site incuding an historic estate, along with an outdoor recreation site that is a delight.

The focus is on contemporary Canadian artwork at the Kelowna Art Gallery. Browsing the wonderful exhibits is a great way to spend an afternoon; watch for annual events you might like as well.

Mission Hill Winery

One of the finest examples of an Okanagan Valley vineyard is Mission Hill Winery. To get the complete experience, plan to take one of the available tours, and of course, the tastings are the best part!

Mission Hill Winery

1730 Mission Hill Road

West Kelowna, BC CA

Myra Canyon Trestle Trail

The Trestle Trail is one of those unique experiences, and a great way to delve into this part of the province. Whether you see it by hiking, biking or on horseback, you are sure to be thrilled with the amazing views and outstanding wildlife who call the park "home".

Myra Canyon Trestle Trail

Central Okanagan, BC CA

Okanagan Lake

Sure, Okanagan Lake is pretty to look at, but it is really fun to play on! While you are in Kelowna, take the opportunity to get out on a boat and try some wakeboarding or fishing. It's a blast!

Okanagan Lake

Silver Star

About an hour and a half's drive brings you to Silver Star Mountain, where you can experience the wintertime recreation you've only dreamed about! From snowboarding to sleigh rides, an amazing terrain park and more restaurants than you could imagine, Silver Star is the perfect destination for your Kelowna getaway.

Silver Star
