Hotel Description

Property Location
Big White Condos by Big White Accomm. offers accommodations throughout the area in Kelowna. &nbsp;

Rooms
Make yourself at home in one of the 138 guestrooms, featuring kitchens with full-sized refrigerators/freezers and microwaves. Complimentary wireless Internet access is available to keep you connected. Conveniences include coffee/tea makers, and housekeeping is provided on a limited basis.

Amenities
Take advantage of recreation opportunities such as ski-in/ski-out access, or other amenities including complimentary wireless Internet access and babysitting/childcare (surcharge). Guests can catch a ride to nearby destinations on the complimentary area shuttle.
