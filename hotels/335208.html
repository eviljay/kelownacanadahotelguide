Stonebridge Lodge

5315A Big White Road, Kelowna, BC CA
Return to Search ResultsNew Search

Hotel Description

Property Location
Located in Kelowna (Big White), Stonebridge Lodge is minutes from Bullet Express and Big White Ski Resort. This ski condo is within close proximity of Telus Park Chair and Lara's Gondola.

Rooms
Treat yourself to a stay in one of the 31 individually decorated guestrooms, featuring fireplaces and private spa tubs. Rooms have private balconies. Kitchens are outfitted with full-sized refrigerators/freezers, stovetops, and microwaves. 40-inch flat-screen televisions with cable programming provide entertainment, while complimentary wireless Internet access keeps you connected.

Amenities
Enjoy the convenience of ski-in/ski-out access at this condo, which also features an outdoor pool and an indoor pool. Additional features at this condo include complimentary wireless Internet access, babysitting/childcare (surcharge), and ski storage. Getting to nearby attractions is a breeze with the complimentary area shuttle.

Dining
Grab a bite to eat at one of the condo's many dining establishments, which include 7 restaurants and 10 coffee shops/cafés. Relax with a refreshing drink at one of the 4 bars/lounges.

Business, Other Amenities
Featured amenities include luggage storage, laundry facilities, and a safe deposit box at the front desk. Guests may use a roundtrip airport shuttle for a surcharge, and free self parking is available onsite.
