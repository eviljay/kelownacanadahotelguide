Okanagan Lake Provincial Park Information and Hotels

Okanagan Lake Provincial Park
Kelowna, BC CA  Phone: 250-548-0076
Okanagan Lake Provincial Park

This popular park, located about a half hour southwest of Kelowna, offers 98 hectares of ponderosa pine, beautiful beaches, and panoramic views of mountains and scenery. As a natural arboretum, you will see a variety of local wildlife and many species of birds.  Activities include canoeing and kayaking, fishing, hiking, cycling, windsurfing and boating. Two large campgrounds are available as well.
